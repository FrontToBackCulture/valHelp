1.  Click on the 'Admin' dropdown.
2.  When the 'Admin' menu appears, click on 'VAL Admin'.
3.  Click on the 'Organisation' tab.
4.  Click on the 'Delete' icon.
5.  Click 'Delete'.
6.  Your Organisation has, now, been erased.
